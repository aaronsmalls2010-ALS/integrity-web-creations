About Integrity Web Creations

A Low Country story of passion, engineering, family, and building digital success for small businesses.

The Person Behind the Work
A

A. Smalls

Founder & Lead Designer

A Low Country native, Computer Engineer, and local entrepreneur who has dedicated his career to making the web work for small businesses — businesses just like yours.

USC Computer Engineering Class of 2010 Beaufort, SC Native 15+ Years Experience

Built From the Ground Up

After graduating from the University of South Carolina with a degree in Computer Engineering in 2010, A. Smalls returned home to Beaufort with a clear mission: the small businesses that make this community thrive deserve a professional, powerful online presence — without being priced out by large agencies that treat them like just another account number.

Integrity Web Creations was built on a simple promise — every client gets personal, undivided attention. You work directly with A. Smalls. There is no hand-off, no junior account manager, no disappearing act. Your business gets the same care and commitment on day 365 as it did on day one.

This is not a large operation and that is entirely by design. Staying small means staying personal. It means your calls get answered, your questions get addressed, and your website always has someone looking after it.

The Journey

The Story So Far

From a laptop in Beaufort to a portfolio of businesses served across South Carolina — and a personal chapter that made everything more meaningful.

2010
Graduated USC — Integrity Web Creations Founded
Armed with a Computer Engineering degree and a clear-eyed view of what small businesses in the Low Country were missing online, A. Smalls launched Integrity Web Creations — with a commitment to personal service and honest work at every budget.
2011 – 2013
Roots in the Community
First clients across Beaufort County. Word spread the old-fashioned way — through satisfied business owners who appreciated working with someone who actually showed up, followed through, and delivered results.
2014 – 2015
Nonprofits & Faith Communities
Expanded into nonprofit and faith-based organizations. The Friendship Baptist Church became a defining project — proving what a community's digital home could look like when built with care and intention.
2016 – 2017
Sports & Professional Organizations
Partnered with the Arena Football Players Union — a milestone that demonstrated the capability to serve organizations at the national professional level and opened new doors across industries.
2018 – 2025
Stepping Away — Family Comes First

"Life reminded me what matters most. From 2018 through 2025, I made the decision to step back from the business and focus on what no amount of work can replace — my family. It was the right decision, and I have no regrets. Some chapters are written for you, not for clients."

— A. Smalls

2026
Back — With More Purpose Than Ever
Returning to the work with renewed perspective, sharper skills, and a deeper appreciation for what small businesses mean to communities like Beaufort. The passion is back. The commitment is stronger. And there has never been a better time to help local businesses claim their place online.
